Frequently asked questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed from your Apple account: Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the period to avoid renewal.

How do I delete my account and my data?
From the App itself: Account screen → delete account. Your profile, plan and messages are deleted. You can also ask us by email.

How do I exercise my data rights (GDPR)?
Write to the contact email. Details are in the Privacy Policy.
