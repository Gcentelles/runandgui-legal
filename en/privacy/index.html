Run&Gui Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This English translation is provided for convenience. In case of any discrepancy, the Spanish version prevails.

This policy explains what personal data we process in the Run&Gui application (the "App"), for what purpose, on what legal basis, and what rights you have. Please read it carefully: the App processes health data, which the GDPR treats as a special category and which we protect accordingly.

1. Data controller

  • Owner: Guillermo Centelles Hidalgo
  • Tax ID (NIF/DNI): 74376234F
  • Address: Galgewater 30, 2311 VZ Leiden, the Netherlands
  • Contact and privacy email: gjcentelles@proton.me

For any question about your data or to exercise your rights, write to that address.

2. What data we process

We collect only what is necessary to prepare your training plan and follow up on it:

a) Identification and contact data Full name, email address, phone/WhatsApp.

b) Physical and sports data Age, sex, weight, height, goal, experience, weekly availability, paces, heart-rate/watch data, and the training plan your coach prepares for you.

c) Health data (special category, Art. 9 GDPR)

  • The PAR-Q physical readiness questionnaire, injuries, and other health matters you declare.
  • If you give your optional consent to menstrual cycle tracking: hormonal contraceptive use, regularity, cycle and bleeding length, date of your last period, heavy bleeding, symptoms and notes; as well as the daily cycle entries you choose to record.

d) Communications The messages you exchange with your coach inside the App.

e) Technical and subscription data Your account identifier, your device's push notification token, and your subscription status (active/expired). Payment is handled by Apple: we do not process or store your card details.

We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do not build profiles from your health data.

3. Purposes and legal basis

Purpose Legal basis (GDPR)
Creating your account and giving you access to the App Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b)
Preparing and delivering your training plan Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b)
Generating and adapting your plan automatically (template engine and AI assistant) Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b)
Processing your health data (PAR-Q, injuries, cycle) to adapt the plan safely Explicit consent (Art. 9.2.a), given separately and revocable
Communicating with your coach (messages) Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b)
Sending you notices (plan published, coach reply) Consent (system notification permission), revocable from your device settings
Managing your subscription Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b) and legal obligations (Art. 6.1.c)

Part of the Service is automated: on the free tier the plan is generated automatically from your profile (template engine); on paid tiers an AI assistant helps adapt it; and on the coach tier, a person reviews the messages before they are sent to you. None of this processing produces legal or similarly significant effects on you (Art. 22 GDPR), and you can always contact your coach. Important: the AI assistant does not receive your health data (neither the PAR-Q nor menstrual cycle data); it is excluded before processing.

4. Consent for health data

Processing your health data requires your explicit consent, which we collect separately and granularly in the App (PAR-Q and menstrual cycle separately) before asking you for that data. You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account or writing to us; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing. Without consent for health data it is not possible to prepare the plan safely.

5. Recipients and data processors

To provide the service we rely on providers that process data on our behalf, under a data processing agreement (Art. 28 GDPR):

  • Supabase (hosting and database). Your data, including health data, is stored on servers in the European Union, encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Apple (App distribution, subscription payment management, and push notification delivery on iOS).
  • RevenueCat (technical management of subscription status). It receives an identifier of your account and subscription data; it does not receive your health data.
  • Expo (push notification delivery service). It processes your device token; it does not receive your health data.
  • Anthropic (AI assistant that generates and adapts the plan). It receives your training profile and your current plan to propose adjustments; it does not receive your health data (PAR-Q or menstrual cycle), which is excluded before sending.

Your coach accesses your profile, your plan and your messages to provide the service.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

6. International transfers

Your health data stays in the European Union (Supabase, EU region). Some technical providers (RevenueCat, Expo, Apple, Anthropic) may process non-health data in the United States: account identifier, device token, subscription status and, in Anthropic's case, your training profile and plan (no health data). Such transfers rely on GDPR safeguards (the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses and/or the EU-US Data Privacy Framework).

7. Retention period

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account (see section 8) or withdraw your consent, we delete your data, including health data. We only keep the minimum information the law requires us to retain (for example, records related to tax or commercial obligations) for the legally required periods, blocked for any other use.

8. Your rights

You can exercise at any time your rights of access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction, portability and to withdraw consent:

  • From the App itself: in Settings › Your account you can export all your data (right of access and portability) and delete your account and all your data (right of erasure).
  • By email: writing to gjcentelles@proton.me. We will reply within one month at most.

Deleting your account does not cancel your Apple subscription; to cancel it, go to Settings › your name › Subscriptions on your device.

If you believe we have not handled your rights properly, you can lodge a complaint with the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (the Dutch data protection authority, autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl), the authority of the country we operate from, or with the authority of your country of residence (in Spain, the AEPD, www.aepd.es).

9. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures: encryption in transit and at rest, EU hosting, and row-level access control (each user can only access their own data). Your health data is not included in diagnostic logs or third-party analytics.

10. Minimum age

The App is intended for people aged 18 or over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under that age.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect legal or service changes. We will publish the current version at this same address and indicate the date of the last update. If the changes affect the processing of your health data, we will inform you and, where appropriate, ask for your consent again.
